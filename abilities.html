Tears of the Kingdom Abilities

Ultrahand

The Ultrahand ability allows the player to manipulate objects from a distance. With this ability, players can interact with objects that are out of reach, solve puzzles, and even combat enemies by grabbing and throwing objects.

Fuse

The Fuse ability grants the player the power to merge with specific objects or creatures in the game world. By fusing with these entities, players gain unique abilities and characteristics. This ability can be crucial for solving puzzles and accessing new areas.

Ascend

The Ascend ability enables the player to scale vertical surfaces effortlessly. With this power, players can climb walls, cliffs, and other obstacles, opening up new paths and allowing for creative exploration. It is a key ability for reaching hidden areas and uncovering secrets.

Recall

The Recall ability allows the player to rewind time and reverse certain actions. It can be used to undo mistakes, retry challenging sections, or reset puzzle elements. The Recall ability provides players with a valuable tool to overcome obstacles and navigate through complex situations.

Autobuild

The Autobuild ability simplifies the process of constructing and crafting within the game. With this ability, players can automatically generate structures, fortifications, or other objects to aid them in their quest. Autobuild enhances the player's creative capabilities and provides convenient assistance during gameplay.
